My life has been a journey of constant change and challenge. Beginning as a typical student in Korea, my pivotal move to Vancouver in 2008 was influenced by family ties. Embracing secondary school life, I cherished the friendships formed. Despite the beauty of Vancouver, my hunger for new experiences led me to the University of Toronto, where I initially pursued Physics. However, as my enthusiasm waned, I fulfilled my mandatory military service, where resilience and professionalism were trained.

Completing my Physics degree amid doubts, I sought new challenges. Working as an administrative manager refined my communication skills. The onset of the COVID-19 pandemic opened unexpected doors, allowing me to explore the world of programming. Despite the risks, I committed to pursuing a degree in Computer Science, a decision that provided purpose for me to pursue.

At school, I thrive on studying algorithms, analyzing data, and collaborating with exceptional individuals to develop innovative applications. Working alongside talented peers, I enjoy the dynamic process of brainstorming ideas, coding, and refining our creations. My passion lies in utlizing technology to resolve real-world challenges and make a meaningful difference in society.

Now, as the graduation approaches, I eagerly anticipate the next chapter, embracing opportunities to shape my future.
